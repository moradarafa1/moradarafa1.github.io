Break the habit with a proven method

Habit Reversal Training is an evidence-based method for changing compulsive habits. Awwad walks you through 4 clear phases, and supports several habits at once (up to 3 to break and 3 to build) that you can switch between.

1) Awareness

Notice and log when, where and why the habit happens, the first and most important step.

2) Competing response

Practice an alternative behavior that blocks the habit at the moment of urge.

3) Environment control

Adjust your surroundings to reduce triggers.

4) Maintenance

Review, keep what works, and protect your progress.

Extra support for compulsive masturbation

If you chose to break this habit, when the urge hits we recommend spending about 15 minutes on the Waai YouTube channel instead of giving in. It is awareness content that helps you understand and stay firm. The app tracks your progress discreetly and suggests practical alternatives for each urge.
