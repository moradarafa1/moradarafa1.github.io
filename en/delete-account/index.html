From inside the app

Open: Settings, then Account, then Delete account. Your account and all data (habits, logs, badges) are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Without signing in

If you can no longer sign in, email a request from your registered address to moradarafa600@gmail.com with subject "Delete account". We verify you via your email, then delete all your data shortly after.

What is deleted

All your personal data, tracking data, trusted devices, and usage records linked to you.
